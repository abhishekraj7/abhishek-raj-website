Early Life

Abhishek Raj was born in Gaya, Bihar, in 1995. He lived near Akshaywat in Maranpur, Gaya, with his family and grandparents in a joint family setup. In 2000, his family moved to Ambala, Haryana, where his father was working at the time.

Academic Journey

Abhishek completed his schooling in various cities, including Ambala, Nalagarh, and Gurgaon. He excelled in his studies, scoring CGPA 10 in his 10th board exams and 89% in his 12th standard. He pursued engineering at UIET Kurukshetra University and later completed his M.Tech at IIT Guwahati.

Professional Career

After completing his M.Tech, Abhishek joined Mavenir Systems in July 2020. He has been working there since, contributing to various projects and gaining valuable experience in his field.
